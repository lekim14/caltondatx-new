Privacy Policy for CaltonDatx

Effective Date: April 04, 2024

At CaltonDatx, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it's used, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect anonymous data related to audience and ad exposure, including:

  • Demographics
  • Emotional expressions
  • Behavior metrics
  • Vehicle data
  • App usage statistics
  • IP addresses

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) or store any data that can directly identify individuals. All data is anonymized and aggregated for analytics purposes only.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Provide real-time audience analytics for Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising
  • Optimize ad campaigns and deliver actionable insights
  • Measure ad effectiveness (e.g., impressions, audience engagement, dwell time)
  • Analyze traffic patterns, vehicle classification, and speed
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Stored securely with encryption during transmission
  • Not linked to PII and retained only for essential durations
  • Not storing images — visuals are processed into anonymous data points (e.g., emotional state, impression count)
  • Protected using industry-standard security protocols such as SSL encryption

4. Data Usage Metrics

We track and analyze:

  • Frequency and duration of app use
  • User interaction with app features (e.g., analytics, reports)
  • App performance for reliability and improvements
  • Engagement metrics for optimizing advertising strategies

5. GDPR and Data Privacy Compliance

We are fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Privacy Act of the Philippines (Republic Act No. 10173). We do not collect any data that can directly identify users. Users can request deletion of any collected data by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party providers for cloud storage and analytics. These providers operate under their own privacy policies, which we recommend reviewing. If third-party tools are used for advertising or analytics, they do so under their own privacy terms.

7. Children's Privacy

CaltonDatx is not intended for children under 13. We do not collect or process data that identifies pedestrians or individuals. If we become aware that data from a child under 13 has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

8. AI-Generated Content Policy (ChatGPT Integration)

CaltonDatx integrates ChatGPT, an AI-powered assistant, to enhance the app experience. This integration:

  • Does not access personal data
  • Only responds to questions specifically about the CaltonDatx app and its features
  • Is restricted from generating or responding to unrelated, offensive, or harmful content
  • Includes user feedback tools (e.g., thumbs up/down) for reporting inappropriate or incorrect AI-generated responses

The AI feature is carefully configured to comply with Google Play’s AI-Generated Content Policy, ensuring it cannot be misused. Users cannot use the app's AI for general inquiries or purposes beyond the scope of the application.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If significant changes are made, we will notify users within the app and/or on our website. We encourage users to review this policy regularly.
